Giko first appeared in 1998 on 2chan, a prominent Japanese
online forum known for its anonymous posting 
and diverse topics that quickly
became a hub for internet culture and creativity. 
Giko was created to represent Shift-JIS art, an ASCII art
form made using Japanese keyboard letters.
